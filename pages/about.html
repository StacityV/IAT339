ABOUT US

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a marketplace for people to buy used electronic devices such as mobile phones, laptops, etc. The website aims to provide a convenient platform for people to trade their used electronics and fully refine the value of second-hand devices. We offer a fully transparent pricing policy where customers can browse all available used equipment on our website and view actual prices. We believe that a transparent pricing policy will help our customers better understand what they are buying.

Our Team

Our team is made up of young people who are interested in developing the pre-owned electronics market. We understand the needs of our customers and make sure that our clients have a good experience when using the platform. Our team is always working to improve and make our platform better while keeping it running well.

Our Target Audience

Our target audience is individuals looking to purchase used electronic devices, such as consumers who are on a limited budget but need electronic devices, including students and young adults who are new to the workforce. Our audience may also include electronics enthusiasts who are obsessed with older devices that are no longer available on the regular market.
